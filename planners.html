Frequently Asked Questions

We'll schedule an onboarding meeting to kickoff planning. We need your time initially to learn about the event and how our college fair can fit within your agenda. We handle the heavy lifting with logistics but will need your team's support in connecting with the appropriate event staff, determining sponsorship opportunities, logistic information like where reps will park, setup times, etc.

The CFC maintains a strong network with universities and colleges. Attendance varies by location and event audience. We set attendance goals that are achievable and can help increase the reach of your event.

We provide a turnkey solution and handle the details so you can focus on the core programming for your event.
