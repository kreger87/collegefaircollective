Dallas Fort Worth Faith-Based College and University Tour

Fall 2026
Dallas-Fort Worth Area

Event Overview

The DFW Faith-Based College and University Tour provides a unique opportunity for faith-based institutions to connect with students who value faith-centered education. This tour visits private and religious high schools throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

Students at faith-based high schools are often seeking colleges that align with their values and beliefs. This tour gives your institution direct access to families who prioritize faith in their college decision-making process.

Tour Highlights

Faith-focused student audience
Catholic & Christian high schools
Values-aligned families
Counselor networking events
Data leads provided via CardCapture
Coordinated logistics support
