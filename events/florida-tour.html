University Tour

Southern Florida Out of State and International University Tour

Engage with diverse student populations in Southern Florida interested in out-of-state and international education options.

Tour Overview

The Southern Florida University Tour connects your institution with students in the Miami and Fort Lauderdale metropolitan areas. This diverse region offers access to motivated students from varied backgrounds who are seeking educational opportunities beyond Florida.

  • Diverse Student Base

    Access one of the most diverse student populations in the United States.

  • Miami & Fort Lauderdale

    Two major metro areas covered in one coordinated tour.

  • Lead Capture Included

    CardCapture technology included for seamless student data collection.
