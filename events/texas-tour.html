University Tour

Texas Out of State and International University Tour

Fall 2026
Dallas, Austin, San Antonio, Houston

Event Overview

Join us for the premier recruitment tour designed specifically for out-of-state and international universities looking to connect with high-achieving students across Texas. This comprehensive 5-day tour covers the four major metropolitan areas: Dallas/Fort Worth, Austin, San Antonio, and Houston.

Participating institutions will have exclusive access to top-tier high schools, district-wide college nights, and counselor networking receptions. This is your opportunity to showcase your institution to thousands of prospective students and build lasting relationships with key influencers in the Texas education community.

Tour Highlights

Access to 15+ top-ranked high schools
Networking with 75+ college counselors
Expected attendance of 3,000+ students
Comprehensive travel logistics support
Data leads provided via CardCapture
Marketing inclusion in student guides
