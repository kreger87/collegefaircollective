University Tour

Houston and Austin Out of State and International University Tour

A dual-city tour focusing on recruitment opportunities in two of Texas's most dynamic education markets.

Tour Overview

The Houston and Austin University Tour brings your institution to students in two of Texas's fastest-growing and most diverse metropolitan areas. This dual-city tour provides efficient access to motivated students looking for educational opportunities beyond their home state.

  • Dual-City Access

    Reach students in both Houston and Austin with one coordinated tour.

  • Diverse Student Population

    Connect with students from diverse backgrounds in these dynamic metro areas.

  • Lead Capture Included

    CardCapture technology included for seamless student data collection.
